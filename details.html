Located under the same roof at Centrio Mall, Seda Centrio features an adults and childrens pool and a fitness centre. The hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi.

Located near the city center of Cagayan de Oro, Seda Centrio is a 3-minute walk to Northern Mindanao Medical Centre. Jesus Nazareno Parish Church and The Museum of Three Cultures are a 10-minute walk away. It takes 45 minutes to drive to Laguindingan Airport.

All rooms come with a flat-screen cable TV, iPod dock and safe. A seating area and private bathroom with shower, hairdryer and free toiletries are included.

The hotel features a 3-in-1 all-day dining at Misto Restaurant serving local and international cuisines. Seda Centrio also offers a coffee lounge.
